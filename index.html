About me

I defended my dissertation on Sep. 30, 2016 and obtained my Ph.D. degree from Rutgers ECE Department. During the 4 years’ study at Rutgers, I was working in the CSPL under surpervision of Professor Athina Petropulu. I obtained my M.S. degree from Peking University in 2012. My advisor is Prof. Yue-Xian ZOU. I received my B.S. degree in Communication Engineering from Lanzhou University in July 2009.

I will join Qualcomm Corporate R&D as a Systems Engineer in December 2016.

Research Interests

  • Wireless communications, Spectrum sensing & sharing

  • Statistical signal processing, detection&estimation, convex/nonlinear optimization

  • Sparse and low rank signal processing: compressed sensing, matrix completion

  • MIMO radar, sensor array

  • Acoustic, audio, speech signal processing
